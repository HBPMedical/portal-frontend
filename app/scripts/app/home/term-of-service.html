HBP MEDICAL INFORMATICS PLATFORM TERMS AND CONDITIONS

I request access to data collected by the Human Brain Project Medical Informatics Platform (HBP-MIP) consortium for the purpose of scientific investigation, teaching or the planning of clinical research studies and agree to the following terms:

1. I will receive access to de-identified and aggregated data and will not attempt to establish the identity of, or attempt to contact any of the participants.

2. I will not further disclose these data beyond the uses outlined in this agreement and my data use application.

3. I will require anyone on my team who utilizes these data, or anyone with whom I share these data to accept and comply with this data use agreement.

4. I will comply with any rules and regulations imposed by my institution and its institutional review board in requesting these data.

5. I will ensure that Investigators who utilize HBP-MIP data use appropriate administrative, physical and technical safeguards to prevent use or disclosure of the data other than as provided for by this Agreement.

6. I will report any use or disclosure of the data not provided for by this Agreement of which I become aware within 15 days of becoming aware of such use or disclosure. Reports/disclosures should be sent via email to hbp_medicalinformatics@chuv.ch.

7. I confirm I have obtained individual access to the databases of ADNI and 3C.

If I publish abstracts or manuscripts using data from HBP-MIP, I agree to the following:

7. I will cite HBP-MIP as the source of data and the DOE funding source in the publication as space allows. Ex: "The imaging data and demographic information was collected and shared by [Centre Hospitalier Universitaire Vaudois, Hôpitaux Universitaires de Genève, Tel Aviv University, Ecole Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne, Josef Stefan Institute, Leiden University Medical Centre, University of Athens, University of Bordeaux, University College London, University of Uppsala, Athens University of Economics and Business] the HBP Medical Informatics Platform supported by the European Commission under Grant Agreement 604102."

8. I will cite XXXX in all reports based on these data, as appropriate.

9. I will include language similar to the following in the methods section of my manuscripts in order to accurately acknowledge data gathering by the HBP-MIP investigators. Depending upon the length and focus of the article, it may be appropriate to include more or less than the example below, however, inclusion of some variation of the language shown below is mandatory.
