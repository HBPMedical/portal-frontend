HBP MEDICAL INFORMATICS PLATFORM TERMS AND CONDITIONS

I request access to data collected by the Human Brain Project consortium for the purpose of scientific investigation, teaching or the planning of clinical research studies and agree to the following terms:

1. I will receive access to de-identified and aggregated data and in such cases will not attempt to establish the identity of, or attempt to contact any of the participants.

2. I will not further disclose these data beyond the uses outlined in this agreement and my data use application.

3. I will require anyone on my team who utilizes these data, or anyone with whom I share these data to accept and comply with this data use agreement.

4. 4. I will comply with any rules and regulations imposed by my institution(s) and its institutional review board in requesting these data.

5. I will ensure that Investigators who utilize HBP-MIP data use appropriate administrative, physical and technical safeguards to prevent use or disclosure of the data other than as provided for by this Agreement.

6. I will report any use or disclosure of the data not provided for by this Agreement of which I become aware within 15 days of becoming aware of such use or disclosure. Reports/disclosures should be sent via email to www.hbp-pore.eu

7. I agree to include the following acknowledgement in any publication where the data set contributed significantly to its content: “Data in this publication were provided by the HBP. The HBP has received funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under grant agreement No 65003.” In addition I agree to include any additional acknowledgement of institutional support or specific funding awards provided in the metadata accompanying the Data Set, including those requested by the Contributor, in any publications where the Data Set contributes significantly to its content.
